Early Life

Tanu was born in Bengaluru, Karnatake, India on May 31, 1996. He is the son of R. Nanda Kumar (Dad) and B. Jyothi (Mom). His ancestors are all indians. His father was a head chef in ITC Canteen, and his mother as a Business and Product Manager at TMEIC, Bangaluru which is one of the finest companies in India. Tanu's grandfather is V Rama Roa, also was a head Chef in ITC Canteen. Tanu do not have any siblings. The family lives in 6th Block, Rajajinagar, Bengaluru. Early in his life, Gates observed that his parents wanted him to become an Engineer.


At the age of 5 Tanu started his school "St Miras High School" which was in Rajajinagar, and was located literally next street of his residence. During his school days Tanu was a really good student both in terms of his academics and his attitude. Most of his class teachers still remember his name.
